Profikio

Connecting teachers with the support they need.

Dear Teachers,

We know being a teacher can be overwhelming, and we are here to help. For now, this means connecting you with an online teaching assistant to help you grade student work. By relieving you of some of your grading, we hope you will have more time to focus on another aspect of your practice that will allow you to make an impact on your students, such as planning that perfect lesson, or really improving your classroom management techniques.

Thank you for making a difference,

Team Profikio
